# Initial Concept

An AI-powered video storyboard generation platform that transforms text descriptions into complete video narratives using Google's generative AI models.

# Product Vision

StoryCraft aims to empower creators by bridging the gap between imagination and visual reality. By leveraging cutting-edge generative AI, it simplifies the complex process of video creation, making professional-grade storytelling accessible to everyone from independent filmmakers to marketing teams.

# Target Users

- **Filmmakers and Storyboard Artists**: Professionals who need fast, high-fidelity pre-visualization and concept art to iterate on their cinematic visions.
- **Marketers and Agencies**: Creative teams looking to rapidly produce high-quality promotional video content with minimal overhead.

# Core Goals

- **Democratize Video Production**: Enable users to generate high-quality, cinematic videos from simple text descriptions, removing traditional barriers to entry.
- **Showcase Google's AI Capabilities**: Demonstrate the power and versatility of models like Imagen, Veo, Chirp, and Lyria in a cohesive, practical application.

# Key Features

- **High-Fidelity Scene Generation**: Precise control over AI models to ensure generated visuals align perfectly with the user's artistic intent.
- **Real-Time Timeline Editor**: A responsive, interactive environment for previewing scenes, adjusting timing, and synchronizing audio.
- **Seamless Video Export**: One-click rendering process that combines scenes, voiceovers, and music into a final professional video file.
